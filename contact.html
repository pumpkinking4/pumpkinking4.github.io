More about me

I was born in 1995 in Utrecht and always lived close-by even though we moved around a lot. Our family always had a love for animals so we had a lot of pets at home, who we all train and play with. Other hobbies of mine are martial arts, I’ve practiced Jiu-jitsu and kickboxing for some years. Gaming was always a big part of my life, I remember getting a N64 for Christmas and being amazed at games like Mario 64 and Yoshi’s story. Soon came the PS1 with Ape Escape and Dragonball Z fighting games I played with my sister. This very quickly evolved into a fascination on how those games were made, watching many making-offs and scribbling in my notebook ‘When I grow up I want to make video games’ when I first could write. Playing games without subtitles is also how I initially learned English, eventually getting a Cambridge certificate for it in high school.

One of my dreams is to create a game that people will play and remember fondly forever, knowing that I contributed to that person’s life by almost literally creating happiness.
